We're looking forward to meeting you!

The official Matrix Conference is a great place for developers, architects, sysadmins, community leaders, from the public and private sector, as well as students and anyone interested in open, secure, decentralised communication.

Venue

The Matrix Conference will happen at Mitosis Labs, in Berlin. The venue is situated in an old factory, repurposed as “LABS” to organise events.

With several levels and an access to the garden, this venue is the perfect place to get together, present new ideas and products, and bond together.

Health & Safety

We’re going to have top notch ventilation, and the place will be wheelchair accessible.

The Code of Conduct of the Matrix.org Foundation applies during the event. We expect attendees, speakers, volunteers and staff to be excellent to each other.
