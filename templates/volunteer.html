Volunteer for the Conference

To keep the ticket price accessible for everyone and provide the best experience as possible, we need volunteers to lend a hand!

Contact us

We are looking for help in those areas:

  • Installing the equipment before the conference
  • Welcoming attendees, scanning tickets and handing badges during the event
  • Helping people with accessibility needs navigate the conference
  • Introducing speakers on stage
  • Handing out pre-ordered merch & selling merch
  • Cleaning up throughout the event
  • Enforcing the code of conduct and health policy
  • Putting the equipment away after the conference

Volunteering for the Conference entitles you to a free ticket, and a limited edition t-shirt and mug. We will create a schedule to make sure volunteers can enjoy the conference too. The more people lend a hand, the less work each one has to do!

If you are interested in making a difference, please email us at conference@foundation.matrix.org
